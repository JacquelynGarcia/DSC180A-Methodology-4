## **Describe a potential investigation you would like to pursue for your Quarter 2 Project.**
A potential investigation that I would like to pursue for Quarter 2 is building an agentic AI systems to detect missing context in articles. Many forms of misinformation are not completely false, they are often missing key details, selectively framed, or incomplete. I want to explore whether an agentic LLM can identify where important context is absent, retrieve the missing information, and generate an augmented explanation. This project would combine RAG, chain of thought prompting, and multi-step planning to create a system that improves the quality of information presented to users.
